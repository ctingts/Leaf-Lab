Welcome to Leaf Lab

Preserving Nature, One Leaf at a Time.

WHO ARE WE?


At Leaf Lab, we guide you through the fascinating process of collecting and preserving plant specimens. Whether you're a botanist, student, or nature enthusiast, you'll find everything you need to turn fresh leaves into preserved history.

NEED SOME HELP IDENTIFYING PLANTS?


Our AI-assisted tool lets you easily identify plants by uploading a photo to our website. It quickly analyses the image, providing the plant's name, care tips, and growth details. Perfect for gardeners and nature lovers, this tool offers fast, accurate insights with just one click.

JOIN OUR COMMUNITY

Do you love plants and want to contribute to the preservation of biodiversity? Join the Leaf Lab community today! By sharing your own herbarium specimens and plant discoveries, you can help expand our database and contribute to plant research worldwide.

How You Can Contribute

  • Share Your Specimens: Upload photos of fresh plant leaves and preserved herbarium specimens.
  • Identify New Species: Use our plant classification tools to help identify rare or unknown species and submit your findings to our database.
  • Collaborate with Experts: Connect with botanists and plant enthusiasts to learn more about specimen preservation techniques and species identification.

Steps to Get Started

  1. Register for a Free Account to join the Leaf Lab community.
  2. Submit your plant specimens using the Contribution Form.
  3. Become a part of our growing community and collaborate on global plant research projects!
