Herbarium Care

Herbarium care involves proper storage, maintenance, and handling of plant specimens to ensure their longevity.

Storage Environment

To ensure your specimens last, follow these guidelines:

  1. Store in a cool, dry place.
  2. Keep away from direct sunlight.
  3. Use a sealed cabinet to prevent damage from pests or humidity.
