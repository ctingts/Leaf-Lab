Fun Fact

  1. Rosa gallica or French rose was one of the earliest rose species to be cultivated from its origins in central Europe.
  2. First domesticated by the Greeks and Romans and later used in Medieval gardens.
  3. Wild plants usually have double-corollas, which make the flowers very showy
